Barbell Squat

How to:

Grip should be close shoulders. Shoulder blades should be squeezed back and down. All four corners of the foot should be firmly planted. Start by pushing hips back and lowering glutes until your quads are parallel with the ground. Push back up through the heels, keeping the back straight, chest up, and knees straight. Maintain firm core throughout. Aim for 6-8 reps

Romanian Deadlift

How to:

Grip the bar slightly more than shoulder-width apart. Keep a tight core and push hips back while keeping a slight bend in the knees. Lower the bar to just under the knees. Push the hips forward by squeezing the glutes to come back up. Repeat for 8-10 reps.

Bulgarian Split-Squat

How to:

With one leg planted on the bench behind you, lower yourself down with control using the forward leg for balance. Push back up using the forward leg. Aim for 6-10 reps per leg

Lying Leg-Curl

How to:

Pull your heels towards your glutes by squeezing your hamstrings and bending at the knee. Pause at the top and slowly lower down with control. Aim for 8-10 reps

Standing Calf-Raise

How to:

Stand on an elevated platform with your toes planted and your heels off the platform. Push your heels up as far as you can and pause at the top. Slowly lower down. Repeat for 10-15 reps.

Seated Calf-Raise

How to:

Push the knee pad up by raising your heels while keeping the toes planted. Pause at the top and slowly lower down. Aim for 10-15 reps.
