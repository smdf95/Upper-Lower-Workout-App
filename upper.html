Barbell Row

How to:

Start by pushing hips back and bending at the waist while maintaining a tight core. Pinch shoulder blades back and imagine squeezing elbows up to the ceiling. Aim for 3 sets of 8-10 reps.

Overhead Press

How to:

Start with feet hip-width apart and tight core. The forearms shout be parallel as you grip the bar. Push the bar up and overhead while making sure to not bend the back. Aim for 6-8 reps.

Lat Pulldown

How to:

Lean back slightly as you pull the bar towards your chest, while imagining that you are pulling your elbows to the ground. Squeeze the shoulder blades together and maintain a tight core. Pause for one second and repeat. Aim for 8-10 reps

How to:

How to:

With a very slight forward bend at the hips, raise the dumbbells up from your side until it is parallel with the ground. Keep a very slight bend in the elbows. Aim for 8-10 reps
